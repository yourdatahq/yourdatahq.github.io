Our Mission

To bring together the data community - data engineers, data ops, data analysts, business analysts, data scientists, machine learning engineers and more through our various efforts

Our Plan

To enable community discovery through our slack workspace and our networking events. And to promote accessibility of the latest tech through our podcast, learning events and tech content

Our Vision

To be a one-stop resource for everything data, analytics and ML. Out of all the rapidly growing streams out there, the data and ML stream needs standardization and collective effort big time, for sustainable progress

What is right for you as a data professional?

Accessiblity and community discovery are kept in mind for each of our efforts.

  • With the slack community, you get to use a familiar messaging tool to connect with other data professionals across the world, join specialized clubs (channels) for NLP, GraphML etc. and start a conversation, get help and learn more. You also get to connect at a personal level with the other data professionals, discover mentors / mentees and more.
  • With the LinkedIn page, you get to extend your use of the professional networking app to stay up to date of our events, podcasts and also discover more like-minded data professionals and add them to your network, discover their companies, referrals and more.
  • With our Podcast, you get to discover expert speakers, topics you haven't thought of before, but now make sense in the world of data and ML and can also give back to the community by becoming an expert speaker yourself.
  • With our curated networking events, AMAs and masterclasses, you get to set aside time to Learn and Connect in a structured way that will help you become a more confident data professional.
